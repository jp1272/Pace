Our Goal

We want to better connect athletes to their coaches, teammates, and other athletes.

Connect with Coaches

Pace allows for coaches to see how their athletes training is going. Letting them see the atheletes training and how they felt about it. We want to make the commmunication gap between you and your athlete as small as possible.

Connect with Teammates

Pace lets you see how your teammates are doing whether you are working out next to them or if you are away. We want you to feel just as connected to you team no matter the where you are in the world.

Connect with other athletes

Finally we want you to be able to connect with athletes from anywhere. Whether you live in the same town, race at the same meets, or just are inspired by their training, Pace allows for you to connect with everyone.

Features

Use Pace for an easy way of keeping up with your training.

Log your workouts manually or have them uploaded instantly.

Pace allows you to be able to upload your workouts from your watch and instantly be able to edit the workout without difficulty. You can also manually log any workout with your notes and stats.

See workouts from your team, friends, or anyone else.

Pace allows you to scroll through workouts posted by your friends and teammates all on one page. You can comment on their workout or just view what they did for the day.

Get feedback from your coaches instantly.

Pace allows your coach to easily see your workouts with all your stats and notes. They can leave feedback on your workout for you to see easier than ever.
